Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by the app store you purchased through:

  • iOS (App Store): open the Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Yumisa → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android (Google Play): open the Google Play Store → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Yumisa → Cancel subscription.

You will keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period. Cancelling a free trial before it ends prevents any charge.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by the app store where you made the purchase:

  • Apple: visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for your Yumisa purchase.
  • Google: open play.google.com → Account → Order history → find Yumisa → Request a refund.

If you need help with the request, email us at support@bureau613.com with your purchase date and order ID.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used for the original purchase, open Yumisa, and go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Your Premium access will be reactivated automatically.

How do I delete my account and all my data?

Open Yumisa and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. We remove your personal data from active systems within 30 days and from backups within 60 days, except where retention is required by law.

You can also email support@bureau613.com from your account email to request deletion manually. See our Privacy Policy for full details on data rights.

How accurate is the AI food recognition?

Typical accuracy is approximately ±15–25% relative to laboratory analysis. Accuracy depends on photo quality, food complexity, portion-size estimation, and ingredient visibility. To improve results:

  • Use good, even lighting — avoid shadows over the plate.
  • Capture the entire meal in frame from above.
  • Include a reference object (fork, hand) so portion size is easier to estimate.
  • For complex dishes, use voice or text entry to describe ingredients.

My photo wasn't recognized. What should I do?

  • Retake the photo with better lighting and a clearer angle.
  • Switch to text or voice entry — just say or type what you ate.
  • Manually adjust ingredients and portion size after recognition if something is off.
  • If the issue persists, email us the photo with a short description — we'll use it to improve the model (only if you opt in).

How do I export my data for a trainer or nutritionist?

Go to Profile → Export, choose a date range and a format (PDF or CSV), then share the file directly via email, messenger, or cloud storage. The export contains your meals, calorie totals, and macro breakdown.

I forgot my password or can't sign in.

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your account email — we'll send a reset link. If you signed up with Apple or Google, use the same provider to sign in. If you still can't access your account, email support@bureau613.com.
